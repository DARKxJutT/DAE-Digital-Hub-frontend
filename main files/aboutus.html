About DAE Digital Library

From concept in 2023 to completion in May 2025 - a revolutionary digital resource for engineering education and research.

Our Mission & Vision

The DAE Digital Library was conceived in early 2023 to address the growing need for accessible, high-quality engineering resources in the digital age. After two years of intensive development, we proudly launched in May 2025 with the most comprehensive collection of digital engineering materials available anywhere.

Our mission is to break down barriers to engineering education by providing free and open access to textbooks, research papers, interactive simulations, and learning tools for students at all levels.

We envision a future where every aspiring engineer, regardless of their location or economic circumstances, can access the resources they need to innovate and solve the world's greatest challenges.
